Translating Stack

It is a technique that helps you translate code or concepts between different programming languages, frameworks, or protocols.

This not only refers to the direct conversion of syntax but also to the interpretation of patterns and solutions, adapting them to new environments or technologies.

Goal

The goal is to facilitate and accelerate the transition between different technologies, optimizing the time an engineer needs to learn a new tool or language, without losing quality or understanding of the code.

Considerations

Training of LLMs

  • LLMs are trained on large amounts of data, mainly from the internet.
  • The quality and quantity of that data are not uniform across all languages and frameworks.

Results Based on Popularity

  • More popular languages and frameworks have more available data, which improves the accuracy of LLMs.
  • Example: Django (Python) has higher precision due to its wide adoption and strong community presence compared to Gin (Golang).

Suggestions

  • Stackoverflow Survey
  • Tiobe Index
  • TIOBE is a company that specializes in measuring and tracking software quality.

Recommendations

1. Having an Expert

It's crucial to have an expert in the target and/or source language or framework to ensure the quality of the generated code.

2. Validation of Results

Always validate the results from Copilot to control and reduce errors that may arise from lack of context or hallucinations.

3. Be clear about the objective of the translation.

If you are clear about what you want to achieve in the process, you will be able to guide Copilot to complete the task efficiently.
